## RECORDING TIPS

1. **Screen Resolution**: 1920x1080 (1080p)
2. **Frame Rate**: 60fps for smooth motion
3. **Audio**: Clear microphone, remove background noise
4. **Pace**: Speak clearly and enthusiastically
5. **Mouse Movement**: Smooth, deliberate
6. **Transitions**: Use quick fades between scenes
7. **Background Music**: Upbeat, modern, cinematic (low volume)

---

## RECOMMENDED TOOLS

- **Screen Recording**: OBS Studio, Loom, or ScreenFlow
- **Video Editing**: DaVinci Resolve, Premiere Pro, or CapCut
- **Audio**: Audacity for voiceover editing
- **Music**: YouTube Audio Library (royalty-free)

---

## QUICK RECORDING CHECKLIST

## EXPORT SETTINGS

- **Resolution**: 1920x1080 (1080p) or 3840x2160 (4K)
- **Format**: MP4 (H.264)
- **Bitrate**: 10-15 Mbps
- **Audio**: 320kbps AAC
